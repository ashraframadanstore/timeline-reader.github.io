FAQ

Common questions

Which feed formats are supported? +

Timeline Reader supports RSS 2.0 and Atom — the two most widely used formats. If a site publishes a feed, Timeline Reader can read it.

What does AI Digest require? +

AI Digest uses Apple Intelligence.

Can I use Timeline Reader without an internet connection? +

Yes — articles already downloaded are available offline. Refreshing feeds and loading new articles requires a connection.

How do I add a feed? +

Tap the + button on the Feeds tab, paste the feed URL, and tap Add. You can also pick from a curated list of popular sources.

Can I listen in languages other than English? +

Yes. Select any installed system voice in Settings → Playback.
